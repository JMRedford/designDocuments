Stats:
Str - Determines physical attack damage.  Some armours and weapons have a minimum str requirement.

Con - Determines HP, HP recovery rate, Endurance recovery, and base physical damage mitigation.
Int - Determines magic damage.  Some magic skills have a minimum int requirement.
Wis - Determines mana regen.
Vit - Determines effectiveness of healing and buffing magic.
